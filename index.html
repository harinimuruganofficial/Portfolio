Research contributions.

International Journal of Innovative Science and Research Technology · April 2024
Clustering Comparison of Customer Attrition Dataset Using Machine Learning Algorithms

In the dynamic landscape of today's business environment, customer retention is a critical factor for sustainable growth and success. This research develops and compares machine learning models for customer attrition and churn prediction using state-of-the-art algorithms: Affinity, Birch, KMeans, and Agglomerative Clustering, evaluating their effectiveness in identifying behavioral patterns. Using a dataset of historical customer data, the study creates prediction models that can help firms proactively address churn concerns and implement targeted retention strategies. The work introduces label selection by evaluating each feature individually via impurity score, and performs cluster classification to determine the optimal cluster by its metrics, delivering businesses predictive analytics capabilities to enhance customer relationship management and inform strategies around loyalty programs, pricing, and service quality.
